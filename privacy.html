Apple Health integration

EnduraScience can write workout summaries to Apple Health if you allow it. The app does not read data from Apple Health.

Storage & sync

Your workout details may be stored on our secure servers in future releases to enable cloud backup and cross-device sync. Any future integrations with services like Garmin Connect or TrainingPeaks will be strictly opt-in.

No ads, no tracking

  • Your data is never shared with third-party advertisers.
  • We do not sell or license personal data.
